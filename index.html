about me

Hi there! My name is Oliver, and I'm a high school student that loves all things creative. My family tells me that I've been taking things apart and putting them back together since I was very young, and I'm inclined to believe them; I've been building my own LEGOs for as long as I can remember (the Technic Bugatti Chiron is a personal favorite), have owned and upgraded my own tenth scale RC since I was in fifth grade, built my own PC when I was in eighth grade, started making the artwork featured on this website when I was a freshman, and have continued my exploration into the STEAM realm ever since. I continue to be particularly passionate about engineering, learning and teaching myself CAD and CAM in Fusion 360, and hope continue into higher education in a mechanical engineering program.


Aside from STEAM, I've been a swimmer for 5 years, and have competed for both my school and a local club. I've also done speech and debate for all my years of high school, and have placed highly at many tournaments at the local, state, and national level. In my spare time (which is few and far between these days) I love to game with my friends; I'm currently ranked bronze in VALORANT (I know I'll get some flack for that haha) and occasionally play Forza Horizon or Assetto Corsa.


Thanks for checking out my website!
